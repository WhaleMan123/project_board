Our Features

Simple Bot with Advanced Tools

WeMakeBull was founded in 2023. Initially, the focus was solely on monitoring wallets within the Ethereum network.

However, as we continued our phased development, it became evident how vital this bot is to the crypto community.

What truly sets it apart and adds the most value for our users are its unique features.

So what are these standout features?

Cryptocurrency Tracking

Add coins by name, ticker or contract address. Apply your personalised settings to receive instant notifications about Price Changes & Swaps.

Price and Change Alerts

Configure alerts based on a specified percentage change in the price of a particular cryptocurrency coin, or set a target price that you want to be notified about.

Swap Alerts

Set $ Value to track Swaps on Uniswap, Sushiswap, Balancer and other supported DEXs.

Token Unlocks

Receive notifications about Token Unlocks from Seed, Private and Other funding rounds for Coins in your Watchlist.

Wallet Tracking

Add a wallet by it`s address to monitor incoming and outgoing transactions, airdrops, NFT transactions and created contracts.

Transaction Tracking

Receive notifications on In/Out Transactions, Swaps, Mints and executed Contracts.

Open Long/Short Positions

Track open Long/Short positions on wallets including position increasing, decreasing or liquidation.

Wallet Balance

Check Balances of assets and NFTs.

NFT Tracking

Add NFT by Collection Name or Contract (Ethereum Network) to track floor prices, changes and unusual Buying or Selling Activity.

Floor Price and Change Alerts

Receive notifications on floor price of your collections and their change.

Pump & Dump Alerts

Enter the number of NFT Buys & sales/per minute that will trigger an alert and do not miss Pump or Dump.

NFT Patterns

Select the number of Unique Addresses from our selection of Whales Lists buying/selling an NFT Collection to trigger an alert.
